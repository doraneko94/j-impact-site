J-IMPACT (the “Organisation”, “we”, “us” or “our”) uses cookies and similar technologies on this website (the “Website”).
This Cookie Policy explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can manage your preferences.

## What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website.
They enable the website to recognise your device and collect information about usage patterns in order to improve functionality and performance.

## Types of Cookies We Use

### Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic operation of the Website, including security and core functionality.

Essential cookies are always active and do not require your consent.

### Analytics Cookies (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to analyse how visitors use the Website.

Google Analytics may collect information such as:

- IP address (processed in anonymised form)
- Pages visited
- Time spent on pages
- Browser and device information

The information collected is anonymised and used for statistical purposes only.
It is not used to directly identify individuals.

Analytics cookies are used only where you have provided your consent.

## Consent and Preference Management

In accordance with EU and UK data protection requirements, analytics cookies are deployed only after your prior consent.

You can manage your cookie preferences through:

- The cookie consent banner displayed on your first visit
- The “Privacy Settings” link available in the footer

You may change or withdraw your consent at any time.

## Google Analytics and International Data Transfers

Google Analytics is provided by Google LLC.

Data collected may be transferred to and processed outside your country of residence, including in the United States.

We take appropriate safeguards in accordance with applicable laws.

## Managing Cookies via Browser Settings

You may configure your browser to block or delete cookies.
However, doing so may affect certain functionalities of the Website.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect legal or operational changes.
The updated version becomes effective upon publication on the Website.
