# J-IMPACT Privacy Policy

- Effective Date: {new Date(frontmatter.date).toLocaleDateString("en-UK")}
- Last Updated: {new Date(frontmatter.update).toLocaleDateString("en-UK")}

J-IMPACT (the “Organisation”, “we”, “us” or “our”) recognises the importance of protecting personal data.
This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use and safeguard personal information obtained through our website (the “Website”).

## Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to personal data collected through the Website.

The Organisation does not conduct direct sales through the Website.
Any product sales are carried out via third-party platforms (such as Leanpub or similar services).
Personal data collected through such external platforms is governed by the respective platform’s privacy policy and terms of service.

## Information We Collect

We may collect the following categories of information:

- Information voluntarily provided via contact forms, including name, email address and affiliation
- Information provided through email correspondence
- Technical data such as IP address, access date and time, and pages viewed
- Information collected through cookies and similar technologies

## Methods of Collection

Personal data is collected when users voluntarily submit information through the Website or when data is automatically collected through cookies or similar technical means.

## Purposes of Use

We use personal data for the following purposes:

- Responding to enquiries and communications
- Operating, maintaining and improving the Website
- Providing information regarding technical materials, research activities, events and publications
- Distributing newsletters and conducting marketing communications
- Preventing unauthorised use and ensuring security

We will not use personal data beyond these purposes unless required or permitted by applicable law.

## Marketing Communications

Where users provide their email address or other contact details, the Organisation may send information relating to publications, technical materials, events or related activities.

Users may opt out of receiving marketing communications at any time.

## External Sales Platforms

The Organisation does not process product purchases directly through the Website.

Sales are conducted via external platforms such as Leanpub.
Any personal data collected through such platforms is managed in accordance with the privacy policies of the respective service providers.

## Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not disclose personal data to third parties except in the following circumstances:

- Where the individual has provided consent
- Where required by law
- Where necessary for service providers engaged to support Website operations

## Security Measures

We implement reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect personal data against unauthorised access, loss, alteration or disclosure.

## Data Retention

Personal data will be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes described in this Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

## Individual Rights

Users may request:

- Access to their personal data
- Correction or deletion of their data
- Restriction of processing
- Withdrawal from marketing communications

Requests may be submitted using the contact details below.

## Cookies

The Website may use cookies for functionality, analytics and performance improvement.
Users may disable cookies through their browser settings, although this may affect Website functionality.

## Children

If a minor provides personal data, such data should be submitted with the consent of a parent or guardian.

## Changes to This Policy

The Organisation may amend this Privacy Policy from time to time.
Updates will take effect upon publication on the Website.
